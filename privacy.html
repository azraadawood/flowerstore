Amour-florist operates as the virtual marketplace. We value the right to privacy as well as this privacy Policy. We commit to protect your privacy when we process your “Personal Information” as defined in the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 ("POPIA") and we set business practices which comply with POPIA.

The terms of this Policy prevail that there is conflict between these policy terms and any other terms in an agreement between us. We might change the terms of this Policy and will process Personal Information according to the latest version.

This Policy addresses any processing of Personal Information as defined in POPIA as it links to our users, customers, visitors of the website or virtual marketplace. The policy provides you with information about our procedures regarding the use, collection and disclosure of Personal Information and it explains the rights you have.

When you use our website, whether to make a purchase, browse it, use our services, login, create an account, we collect personal data from you. Please note that if you choose not to give personal information when it is required for us to process your order or in accordance with a legal requirement, we will not be able to accept or process and fulfil your order.

The personal data which we collect, store or process includes the following:

We collect personal data that you give when you purchase from us, your contact details when creating an account, this includes your name, surname, email, address and phone number, delivery details, details that is associated with the products you order, when you make a purchase, payment card details are passed through to a secure payment gateway and is not held by Amour-florist and we collect your communication and marketing preferences. We collect personal data collected when you use our Website such as information of your online browsing activities on our Websites, this includes the pages visited and details of the type of device used to access our Website and your device IP address.

Please notify us, if you believe we have collected personal data from a person under the age of 18. We do not collect or use children's Personal Information.

We use your personal information for internal business purposes, we will use the personal data collected to help us understand how many people visit our website, if the website is working and to consider improvements which we might need to make to the website to improve your online experience, this also helps us determine what products and services you are interested in and what you or other customers might purchase from us in future.

We may collect our Personal Information directly from you, this is when you give it to us, such as when you create an account to use our services as a Customer, contact us or through the course of our relationship with you, from public sources such as on social media or online platforms, where your Personal Information is public, from the use of our services or use of any features or resources available on or through our services and from third parties which was entitled to share your information with us or you have given consent for them to share your information.
