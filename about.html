About us
Hi there, it is nice to meet you.
I am Amanda Scott and I started my journey in 2003.
I had always been intrigued by the beauty of flowers, their vibrant colours and enchanting scents. My childhood was spent in the wildflower grasslands that surrounded my home, I use to explore the flowers and always use to dream that one day I will share my love for flowers with the world.
The love and passion I had for flowers blossomed into a deep-seated ambition as I grew older. I decided turn my dream into reality. My heart overflowing with love for blooms and an unwavering entrepreneurship, I started my journey to launch my own flower store, “Amour-florist.”

Amour-florist sells fresh flowers that are handpicked and exquisite. From elegant roses to joyful daisies, every flower whispers a tale of love and joy. I dedicated my heart into hand-selecting each flower as well as designing the ideal floral arrangements. I wanted Amour-florist to be more than just a store to buy flowers, I wanted it to be a place where people could celebrate moments which are special and express their emotions through the language of flowers. The flowers that Amour-florist sells touch the hearts of everyone that sees them, Whether it is a bouquet for a dinner party, a wedding or a gesture of gratitude.

Amour-florist prospered beyond its success, the beauty of my store lies in the smiles that it brought to others, the tears that it dried and the love it gave. Amour-florist is more than just a store, it is a place of beauty, love and unlimited possibilities.

It has been an amazing journey so far, Amour-florist is motivated for growth because we want more. More for our florists. More for our customers.

Here it what we do:
Amour-florist makes it simple and easy for you to purchase the most beautiful flowers those you love. Every time you purchase from Amour-florist, you are expressing care to someone. This is why each order is done with the upmost attention to detail.

Why Amour-florist?
The French word "Amour" translates to "love" in English. The term "Florist" is someone who sells flowers and floral arrangements. Amour-florist means "Love Florist" or "Flower Shop of Love." It is a store where flowers are not only sold as products but they represent love, passion and care. It evokes the idea of a flower boutique that customers can convey their affection through the lovely appearance and language of flowers.

Amanda Scott, Ceo of Amour-florist
